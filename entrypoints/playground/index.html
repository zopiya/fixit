How to use FixIt

  1. Press Alt + F to toggle annotation mode.
  2. Hover over any element to see a blue highlight.
  3. Click an element to open the annotation bubble.
  4. Type your comment and press Enter to save.
  5. Open the Side Panel to see all annotations.
  6. Click Copy AI Work Order to generate a Markdown work order.
